The Attendance Problem, Solved.

Manual attendance is a tedious, time-consuming process that takes away from valuable classroom time. Teachers are forced to call out names, which can be disruptive and prone to human error. Attendrix revolutionizes this process by leveraging the power of Artificial Intelligence. Simply take an image of your class, and our system instantly identifies each student, marking their presence automatically. This not only eliminates the hassle of manual checks but also provides accurate, real-time data, allowing educators to focus on what matters most: teaching. With Attendrix, a task that once took minutes now takes mere seconds.
